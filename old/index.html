About

My name is Andrew and I am a master's student studying computer science at the University of Michigan's College of Engineering. My professional interests lie in web development, autonomy, and robotics and it is my goal to use these passions to positively impact the lives of others. I am currently exploring these interests by contributing to the Michigan Mars Rover Team (MRover), where I am currently the lead software technical advisor, and by helping teach Web Systems (EECS 485). I have extensive experience with user interfaces and object oriented programming. My most recent pet project was rebuilding the MRover Navigation Simulator and my most recent work experience was interning at Amazon. In my free time I enjoy watching sports, especially hockey and college football, working on DIY projects, and playing sub-par golf.
